Episode 2: The Learning Journey

My journey has never been just about learning code… it has been about understanding how technology can solve real-world problems.

I started with the basics — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Gradually, they stopped being just languages and became a medium for expressing my creativity.

As I explored further, I stepped into the world of React, building dynamic and interactive web applications.

But my curiosity didn't stop there… I began exploring the world of data.

With Python, Pandas, and Machine Learning tools, I learned to identify patterns and transform data into meaningful stories.

Every project, every concept I learned has made me more confident, more capable, and even more curious to grow.
